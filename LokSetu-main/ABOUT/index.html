01 — Mission

Solving the
Core Problem

A Secure & Scalable Blockchain-based E-Voting System to eliminate logistical barriers and provide a tamper-proof audit trail for India's democratic process.

01

Zero Logistical Barriers

Enable remote voting for migrant populations without requiring physical presence at home constituencies.

02

End-to-End Encryption

Votes are encrypted client-side using hybrid RSA+AES, decrypted only by the blockchain smart contract during tallying.

03

Tamper-Proof Audit

Every transaction recorded on a distributed ledger, providing an immutable trail for 100% transparency.

03 — Security

Eliminating The
"Ghost Voter"

One person. One vote. Forever guaranteed.

AI Liveness Detection

AI analyzes micro-movements to ensure the person is physically present — preventing photo-spoofing and deepfake attacks.

Immutable Blockchain Ledger

Once cast on Hyperledger Fabric, votes are permanently locked. No alteration, no deletion — ever.

Zero-Knowledge Proofs

Verify voter eligibility without exposing any sensitive identity data on-chain. Privacy by design.

Handling 1.4 billion people requires more than a database — it requires an ecosystem.

Terminals send data in "Heartbeats". Kafka queues millions of concurrent requests instantly — zero lag, zero drop.

Edge Computing
